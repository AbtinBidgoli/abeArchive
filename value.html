~FAQ~

  • Overview

  • Which platform will you use for my website?

    I personally code pages developed from HTML markup, CCS stylesheets for styling and unobtrusive JavaScript for behaviors on Github.

  • What costs should I expect other than website development?

    Domain ~ I get my domains from Google because they’re so easy to work with.
    SEO ~ I prefer SEMrush, an advanced Search Engine Optimization tool.

  • What if I don’t have the money to invest in a website right now?

    Money is personal & I know investment decisions can be difficult. I run a small business as well.
    Doing the same things that aren’t getting you results is more expensive than taking action & fixing your website.
    Websites promote you 24/7, no employee can do that.

  • What if I’m not sure I’m ready to invest in a website?

    Do you have a clear niche? A mission statement? What value do you provide for your clients?
    If you answered “yes” to the questions, you’re ready.

  • Do you offer payment plans?

    Yes! For every website, the payment is split into 4 parts:
    20% deposit to confirm your slot in my calendar, 24% at the start of the project, 24% at the midpoint of the project, & 32% at the end of the project.

  • Who owns the content on my website?

    You own everything. I will assign all intellectual property rights when I transfer ownership to you.

  • Do you offer ongoing support after the project is over?

    Absolutely. At the end of the website development project, we can discuss how I can continue to support your business.

  • What is the scope of the process?

    The project will commence with a discovery process where I ask you questions to get to know your business and brand. We get super clear about what you do and why.
    I write all the words for your website, making sure it sounds like you. I develop your website in Github, checking in with you throughout the process.
    I walk you through your website, we go live and start telling your audience!
