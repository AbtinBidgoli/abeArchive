~FAQ~

Which platform will you use for my website?
~ I code pages from HTML markup, CSS cascading stylesheets & unobtrusive JavaScript for behaviors on Github.~

What’s the average cost for website development?
~ The final price is directly related to the project scope, timeline, & deliverables. Project scope can’t be determined without a strategy brief, so the first step is the discovery process. Once we’ll have clear overview, simply & concisely detail the project’s creative & communicative objectives as understood, I’ll be able to propose a final price, deadline & authorization to proceed. For now I can only give you a ball-park range, the clients I partner with typically come to me with more than $1,000 & less than $10,000 in their budget.~

What costs should I expect other than website development?
~ Domain & SEO, Google Domains powered by Google reliability, security & performance & SEMrush, an advanced Search Engine Optimization tool.~

What if I don’t have the money to invest in a website right now?
~ Money is personal & investment decisions are hard. Doing the same things that aren’t getting you results is more expensive than taking action & fixing your website.
It’s like having a top-performing sales rep working 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Websites promote you 24/7, no employee can do that.~

What if I’m not sure I’m ready to invest in a website?
~ Do you have a clear niche? Mission statement? What value do you offer to your clients?
If you have a clear answer to all the questions, you’re ready.~

Do you offer payment plans?
~ Yes payment is split 4 ways:
20% deposit to book your slot in my calendar
24% at the start of the project
24% 3 weeks after the initial discovery meeting
32% at the end of the project.~

How long will take you to revamp our website?
~ It depends on the project scope. But from previous experience, designing & developing new landing page can take up to 2 weeks, while more complex websites can take up to 4-6 weeks.~

Who owns all the content on my website?
~ You own everything. I will assign all intellectual property rights when I transfer ownership to you.~

Do you offer ongoing support after the website is done?
~ Absolutely we can discuss how I can continue to support you after we go live.~

Where are you based?
~ I live in LA but you don’t have to worry about time zones. I contact clients from all over the world, from Dubai to San Diego to Toronto.~
